Our data files used to develop the ALM model are sourced from several other parties.  

Data sources: 

COVID-19 cases were collected daily on a per-US-county basis and shared via usafacts.org website at https://usafacts.org/visualizations/coronavirus-covid-19-spread-map/.

The consumer price index is studied at the St. Louis Federal Reserve Bank and published monthly FRED economic data. https://fred.stlouisfed.org/categories/9,
2023.

Cybersecurity vulnerabilities are studied and collected by the Mitre Corporation and made available publicly with CVE:  https://cve.org, 2024.

The US Bureau of Reclamation measures Lake Mead monthly to determine how much water was lost or gained and shares that information with the public https:
//www.usbr.gov/lc/region/g4000/hourly/mead-elv.html 2024.

Social media data was collected from the GDELT project at https://www.gdeltproject.org 2024.  We filtered data counts of Covid-19 content to produce count data found within the data repository

CovidHub data includes the forecasts of various methods and makes data avalible at: https://covid19forecasthub.org/ 2024
